Website Help

Please select from one of the many options below to view helpful information pertaining to specific issues within the website.

What is this website?

This is a website designed for students to obtain personal or academic information.

How can I view my personal information?

To view your personal information, click the "PERSONAL" tab in the header of the website. This will redirect you to a webpage displaying your personal information.

What does my personal information contain?

Your personal information page contains a variety of helpful identifying information. This includes:
  1. Your 8-digit unique identification number.
  2. Your full legal name given to you at birth.
  3. Your University email address for communications.
  4. Your full street address and place of origin.
  5. Your home phone number.
  6. Your date of birth, including the corresponding timezone.
  7. Your social security number. Don't worry, it's secure.

How can I view my academic information?

To view your academic information, click the "ACADEMIC" tab in the header of the website. This will redirect you to a webpage displaying your academic information.

What does my academic information contain?

Your academic information page contains a variety of helpful information related to your academics at the University. This includes:
  1. Your 8-digit unique identification number.
  2. Your full legal name given to you at birth.
  3. Your University email address for communications.
  4. Your full street address and place of origin.
  5. Your home phone number.
  6. Your date of birth, including the corresponding timezone.
  7. Your social security number. Don't worry, it's secure.

How can I view my class information?

To view your class information, click the "CLASSES" tab in the header of the website. This will redirect you to a webpage displaying your class information.

What does my class information contain?

Your class information page contains a variety of helpful information related to the courses you are currently enlisted in. This includes:
  1. A list of all courses you are currently taking.
  2. The identifying CRN number for each course.
  3. The official class title for each course.
  4. The professor name for each course.
  5. The number of credits for each course (0-4;P/NP).
  6. The location for each course.
